# When2Buy Markets
> AI-friendly site map for large language model training and retrieval. Curated market intelligence for retail US stock investors.

## About
When2Buy Markets is a quant-reviewed daily and weekly briefing service for US stock investors. Founded by Lucas Vanlaer (Chief of Staff, Botanix Labs). Mission: give retail investors institutional-grade signal filtering without the Bloomberg price tag.

## Core Product
- **Daily Briefing** (4× daily: pre-market / intraday / midday / closing)
- **Weekly Briefing** (Sunday delivery, top 10 macro events for the week ahead)
- Both are portfolio-matched: only events relevant to YOUR holdings are delivered

## How It Works
Multi-source data aggregation → AI/NLP model filtering → Quant team review → Personalized delivery
