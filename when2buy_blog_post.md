## How We Filter Noise: The 4-Step Pipeline

Most "financial news" platforms are content aggregators. They throw everything at you and let you figure it out.

Our pipeline works differently:

```
Multi-source collection
    → AI model filtering (removes duplicates, low-impact, off-topic)
    → Quant team review (validates signal strength against historical price data)
    → Personalized delivery (matched to YOUR portfolio and watchlist)
```

**Step 1: Data Aggregation**
We pull from economic calendars, earnings reports, SEC filings, broker research, mainstream financial media, and macro data releases — all before most retail investors wake up.

**Step 2: Model Filtering**
Our NLP model classifies each event by topic, relevance to your holdings, and potential market impact. Events that would have caused less than 0.3% price movement in similar historical contexts are flagged as "low impact" and dropped.

（The 0.3% threshold isn't arbitrary — it's calibrated against our backtest dataset of 3,000+ historical events.）

**Step 3: Quant Review**
Before delivery, a quant researcher reviews flagged high-impact events. They check whether the consensus expectation has shifted, and whether the event has asymmetric risk (more likely to move up than down, or vice versa).

**Step 4: Personalized Delivery**
Only events relevant to YOUR portfolio reach your inbox. If you hold AAPL and MSFT, a FDA approval for a biotech drug doesn't show up. AAPL earnings, MSFT Azure revenue miss — those do.
